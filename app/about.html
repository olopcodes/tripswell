Our Story

It all started on a dating app. Scrolling through the app, numerous swipes, I became aware of similar pictures in people's profile. I began to think back at my travels and that of my friends

Turns out we all had similar experiences when we travel. Thinking that there must be something we are missing when we travel. We are stuck with the primary tourist traps and nothing novel occurs on our trips

And this is how tripswell was born, a place where travels can visit other places and have unique experiences not the same circulated one. I hope you view more of our trips and continue to tripswell my friends!
